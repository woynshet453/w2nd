woldiya universty in ethiopiaWoldia University is a public university in Ethiopia, established in 2011 in the city of Woldia. It is a higher education institution with a focus on teaching, research, and community service, offering a diverse range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and PhD programs across various fields. The university serves as a significant center for education in the Amhara region, with three main campuses: the main Woldia Campus, Mersa Agricultural College, and St. Lalibela Institute of Heritage and Tourism Studies.
